üîÑ Reroll Methods

üìã Method Selection
Choose the primary method the bot will use for rerolling accounts (e.g., 13 Pack method, Inject method).

13 Pack
Creates brand new accounts, completes beginner missions and opens 13 packs. Select this option to start creating a repository of accounts to reroll for wonder picking.
Recommendation: Depending on how fast your machine is and how long your machine will be rerolling, create approximately 300 - 1000 accounts per instance you plan to run.
Inject
Logs into your saved accounts from your account repository, opens 2 daily packs. Used to "train" your accounts to the 39 pack count requirement for the accounts to show up in wonder pick.
Inject Missions
Used for accounts that have been previously saved, but did not complete beginner missions. Prior to restricting reroll methods to 13 Pack, accounts saved as 3 Pack and 5 Pack are eligible to run this setting.
Inject for Reroll
Once accounts have opened 39 packs, they are eligible to be found in Wonder Pick. This method will log into a saved account, add friends, open two (or three) packs, scans the pack for user-defined desirable cards, if a desirable card is found, it does not remove you as a friend so you may try to test and see if will appear in Wonder Pick. Otherwise it will unfriend you and move on to the next account.
